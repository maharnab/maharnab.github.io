After years of letting the old website sit unchanged, I rebuilt the entire site on Astro.

The goal is simple:

- Keep the site static, lightweight, and fast.
- Make portfolio pages and the blog work as one coherent public profile.
- Keep writing friction low so research notes can be published quickly.

This version keeps the structure minimal but modern enough to evolve over time.
