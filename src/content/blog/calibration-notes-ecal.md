A recurring challenge in calorimeter work is balancing throughput with calibration quality.

A practical baseline workflow I rely on:

1. Validate channel integrity and pedestal stability before full runs.
2. Build calibration constants from cosmic muon response across modules.
3. Re-check consistency after integration changes and transport.
4. Keep software pipelines reproducible for future reprocessing.
